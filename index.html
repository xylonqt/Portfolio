About Me

Hello! My name is Marjun Daniel Lambino, and I am a passionate programmer and a dedicated origami artist. I specialize in C++ and Web develpoment, and I also create intricate and unique origami pieces.

I am currently studying in Exact Colleges of Asia, where I'm developing a strong foundation in various programming languages, including Python, JavaScript, and C++.

Alongside my programming studies, I have cultivated my love for origami. This ancient art form has taught me patience, precision, and creativity. I enjoy experimenting with new designs and pushing the boundaries of traditional origami.

My unique combination of technical expertise and artistic skills allows me to approach problems from multiple angles and create innovative solutions. I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow both as a programmer and an artist.

Please take a moment to explore my portfolio, and feel free to reach out if you have any questions or would like to collaborate on a project.
